Arabicly: Learn Arabic Online

Greetings fellow learners!

Learning is a lifelong journey, and we believe our Arabicly program can assist you in making it possible.

Arabicly is a comprehsenive educational online platform; it provides beginner, intermediate, advanced lessons, exercises, and tools to track your progress. Learning a language can be tricky and we understand that, we are constantly coming out with new ways to make learning Arabic as simple as possible. This online platform goes over the Arabic Alphabet, short + long vowels, special characters, and how to connect letters; all which seems hard at first but we’re making it fun and easy to learn! Keep reading below for more Arabicly news!

As you know, teaching and learning Arabic takes time; to help with that Arabicly is taking time to make sure we implement all of your needs. Here is what we’ve accomplished thus far in our fun learning program!

  • Mobile friendly website to learn on the go
  • High quality audio + Modern Standard Arabic curriculum
  • Charts to track learning progress
  • Over 20+ different lessons available
  • Game like lessons
  • 24/7 online customer support

And we have something for teachers as well…..

  • Create and monitor classrooms
  • Assign homework
  • Send homework email reminders
  • Review and track student’s progress

Or if you’re a parent trying to teach your child Arabic

  • Assign homework
  • Track progress

Check it out at Learn Arabic and let us know what you think!
